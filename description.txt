Use this mod to modify vanilla's uniformed distribution!
Most significantly influenced will be world generation.
The other aspects like item loot will change a lot as well.
Have fun ;P
Press F to open the editor in world by default.
Multiplayer not support.

The character of the icon is "Xeno a", from Len'En Project.
